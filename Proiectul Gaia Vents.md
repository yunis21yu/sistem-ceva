# Project Gaia Vents: Global Submarine Geothermal Grid

## 🌍 The Vision (Viziunea)
This is a conceptual project open to the world. The goal is to design a global network of submarine geothermal power plants built directly on tectonic rifts and hydrothermal vents ("black smokers"). Instead of fighting earth's tectonic energy or letting it accumulate into destructive earthquakes, we propose harvesting this infinite, clean thermal energy to power humanity and eliminate resource-based suffering.

## 💡 How It Works (Cum funcționează - Simplu)
1. **The Source:** Hydrothermal vents at the bottom of the ocean release water at over 400°C under immense pressure.
2. **The Capture:** Sealed, pressure-resistant capsule turbines are placed over these vents.
3. **The Grid:** The extreme temperature difference between the vent (400°C) and the deep ocean water (2°C) drives a highly efficient binary-cycle turbine, sending clean electricity to land via subsea HVDC cables.
